Voyant Visualization Project


For my voyant visualization project, I chose four novels by the English novelist Charles Dickens:

  1. David Copperfield
  2. Bleak House
  3. The Pickwick Papers
  4. Nicholas Nickleby

I first arranged the novels by date and then searched for the following five words “family,” “friends,” “home,” “house,” and “love.” By using the trend tool in Voyant (as shown below), I noticed that the use of these terms gradually increases from Dickens’s earlier to later novels. The increase in these terms may be a reflection of Dickens’s social life, where, as he grew older, he became more interested in these issues.
